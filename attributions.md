# [Text-Based-Game](https://jgouken.github.io/text-based-game) (c) Jgouken, SavorySam
<img width="1919" height="1079" alt="Screenshot 2026-01-31 194744" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2457f0c2-9549-449a-afbd-08971a763612" />

Text-Based-Game is a turn-based text-based rpg with over 100 items, unique crafting and synergies, and much much more held entirely on an html webpage. Play on any device, and at any time, and even save your progress across devices.



## AI Usage
No AI assets were intentionally used on any part of this website.<br>
No AI tools, except for GitHub's "GitHub Copilot Chat" Visual Studio Code extension (Inline Suggestions only), were used.

## Assets
All enemy assets are made by MoBroPro.
Most other assets are made by Jgouken.

Background assets were found or made on Freepik or Vecteezy, filtered from AI use, and are attributed below.
Images have been amateurly inspected for use of AI, but we make mistakes. If a piece of content is found to be AI generated, it will be removed.
